Who Are We?

Amica helps students improve their health wellbeing and increase productivity. As it provides the students with a full health environment from challenges, tests, exercises, and live sessions to how to deal with their mental health, and one on one sessions (Physiological – Nutrition - Counseling) to realize their stress, and finally, if they need a specialist, we will provide them.

Our Vision

“We aim to improve the well-being of students, relieve their stress and enhance awareness about students' mental health.”

Our Mission

“Aiding students to lead a good life academically and socially”
